☕ Coffee Shop Sales Dashboard

An interactive Coffee Shop Sales Dashboard built using Excel, Power BI, SQL, and data visualization techniques to analyze sales performance, customer purchasing behavior, product trends, and store-level insights. The dashboard transforms raw transaction data into actionable business intelligence, helping stakeholders make data-driven decisions.

📊 Key Features
Sales Performance Overview
Total Sales: ₹6,98,812
Average Bill per Customer: $4.69
Average Orders per Customer: 1.44
Time-Based Sales Analysis
Hourly order quantity trends to identify peak business hours.
Weekly order distribution to understand customer traffic across weekdays.
Monthly revenue analysis showing sales growth and seasonal trends.

📈 Business Insights Generated
Identified peak sales hours between morning and mid-day periods.
Determined highest-performing store locations based on revenue and order volume.
Analyzed customer preferences across beverage and bakery categories.
Tracked monthly revenue trends to support inventory and staffing decisions.
Highlighted top-selling products for promotional and marketing strategies.
🎯 Project Objective

To provide a comprehensive view of coffee shop operations by combining sales, customer, product, and location data into a single interactive dashboard, enabling faster decision-making and improved business performance.

Dashboard Type: Sales Analytics & Business Intelligence
Domain: Retail / Food & Beverage
Use Case: Revenue Tracking, Product Performance Analysis, Customer Behavior Insights, Store Performance Monitoring
